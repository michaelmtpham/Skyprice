What is Skyprice?

Skyprice is a Skyblock companion app meant to enhance your experience and take many boring tasks off your hand. In my experience playing Skyblock, many of these tasks weren't particularly interesting, which is my primary motivation for creating this. I hope that it benefits you as much as it benefits me!
